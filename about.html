About page

Discover Our Journey, Vision, and Team

Over the past 50 years, we have become a prestigious and leading network of bookshops in Sri Lanka, expanding our business activities both in terms of quality and quantity. We offer a vast collection of Sri Lankan and foreign publications covering a wide range of disciplines to cater to the diverse needs of our readership, from children to academics. Our network also offers a variety of educational items, stationery, and magazines. We are trusted sellers of an extensive collection of products, and our strong relationships with renowned foreign publishers ensure that we always have the latest publications available for our customers.
